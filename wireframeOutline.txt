[REQUIRED] Project Description: 
The Travel Docs project allows users to quickly and simply view required documents for travelling from one location to another. Whether the user is a first time international traveller, confused with all the frequently changing restrictions, or does not want to read lengthy government webpages, the user will have a resources to figure out the requirements and travel with confidence.
